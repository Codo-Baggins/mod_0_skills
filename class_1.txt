class: Dish


attributes:

sideDish (string)
ingredients (array)
cookTime (float)
isSpicy (boolean)


methods:

changeSideDish (changes the sideDish attribute)
makeVegetarian (replaces the meat ingredient string in the ingredients array with a "vegetarian alternative")
wellDone (adds 5 to the cookTime attribute)
makeSpicy (adds "hot sauce" string to ingredients array and changes isSpicy attribute to true)
